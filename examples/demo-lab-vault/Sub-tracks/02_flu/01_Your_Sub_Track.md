# 01 · Your sub-track — flu cytokine panel reading

**Deliverable:** a reproducible pipeline that turns a figure panel into a verified, provenance-tracked
value + a `FIGURE_FINDINGS` entry, for the H9N2 cytokine figures.

**Why it matters:** the real fold-change numbers live in the figure panels, not the text. If the lab
can trust figure-derived values, they can feed models and grants. You are upstream of all of that.

**Success looks like:** every value traceable to its panel crop; clean panels `verified`, messy ones
honestly `needs-review`. See `00_START_HERE.md` for the phased plan + supervision gates.
